This website aims to educate and encourage people to explore the rich history of video games, showcasing iconic titles, legendary consoles, and the evolution of gaming culture across different eras.

Who I Am

Hi, I’m Omar Al Salti. I’m studying Computer Science at MCBS, with a minor in Cybersecurity. I’ve always been interested in video games and how they’ve changed over the years. This website is part of my ICT 128 – Web Application Techniques course project, taught by Ms. Maryam Al Washahi. I’m still learning web development, so this project has been a good chance for me to experiment, make mistakes, and slowly get better. It’s a simple site, but I tried my best with it. I made this page to share some of the consoles, games, and moments that helped shape gaming. If anything here feels interesting or nostalgic to you, then that honestly makes me happy. Thank you for checking out my website, it really means a lot.

Omar Al Salti
(MCBS Student)

Website Objectives

  1. Encourage people to explore the history of video games.
  2. Showcase iconic games and consoles.
  3. Compare different eras of gaming.
  4. Highlight the cultural impact of gaming.
  5. Demonstrate how the gaming scene evolved.
  6. Preserve gaming history for future generations.
