Surprising Gaming Facts!

Here are some cool facts!

The PlayStation Was Almost a Nintendo Console

Sony originally worked with Nintendo on a CD-ROM add-on for the SNES. That partnership failed… and Sony decided to make their own console. The PlayStation was born out of pure spite.


Minecraft’s First Version Was Built in Under a Week

Minecraft’s creator made the first playable version in only six days. It grew into one of the biggest games ever… from less than a week of coding.


The PS2 Sold Over 155 Million Units

The PS2 became a global hit not just for games many people bought it because it was the cheapest DVD player at the time!


Xbox’s Name Started as a Tech Joke

The original name was “DirectX Box” because it showcased Microsoft’s DirectX graphics tech. The team shortened it… thankfully.


Pac-Man Was the First Character With Lore

Pac-Man actually had an official personality and story, long before Mario or Sonic. He loves eating and dislikes ghosts. Very relatable :D
