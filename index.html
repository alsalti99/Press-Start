The meaning behind our logo!

The logo embodies a powerful gaming aesthetic with the website name "Press Start" displayed at its center. This phrase immediately shows classic gaming nostalgia, reminiscent of the iconic start screens from vintage arcade machines and consoles.

Key design elements include:

  • The Red Pipe: An iconic gaming symbol representing secret paths and hidden areas, encouraging player exploration and rewarding curiosity - core values of our platform
  • Handheld Consoles: Integrated into the design to represent portability and accessibility, honoring their crucial role in making gaming personal and mobile

Together, these elements create a visual identity that celebrates gaming's rich history while embracing its evolving nature.
