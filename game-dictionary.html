Popular Gaming Terms!

FPS

First-Person Shooter — a game where you see through the character's eyes and use weapons.

RPG

Role-Playing Game — games where you build a character and progress through stories and quests.

Indie

Independent developer or game not published by a major company.

Emulator

Software that allows you to play games from older consoles on modern devices.

AAA

High-budget, high-quality game made by a major studio.

NPC

Non-Playable Character characters controlled by the game, not the player.

Boss Fight

A challenging battle against a strong enemy, usually at the end of a level.

Open World

A big game world you can explore freely without strict paths.

Lag

Delay between your action and the game responding — usually from bad internet.

OP

Overpowered: when something in the game is way too strong.

Respawn

When your character returns after being defeated.

Clutch

When a player saves the game or wins at the last second.
