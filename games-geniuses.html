Shigeru Miyamoto

Nintendo — Creator of Mario & Zelda

The father of modern gaming who created Super Mario, The Legend of Zelda, and many Nintendo classics. His focus on fun-first gameplay reinvented the industry.

Hidetaka Miyazaki

FromSoftware — Creator of Dark Souls

Miyazaki reinvented difficulty with the Souls series (Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Elden Ring). His intricate worlds and tough-but-fair design reshaped modern action games.

Hideo Kojima

Creator of Metal Gear Solid

A cinematic game director known for Metal Gear Solid and Death Stranding. Kojima blends storytelling, technology, and gameplay in groundbreaking ways.

Valve

Creators of Half-Life, Portal & Steam

Valve shaped both PC gaming and game design with, Portal, and the Steam platform the backbone of modern PC game distribution.

Naughty Dog

Creators of Crash Bandicoot & The Last of Us

From fun PS1 platformers to cinematic story-driven hits, Naughty Dog crafted Uncharted and The Last of Us, redefining emotional storytelling in games.

Sega

Creators of Sonic & pioneers of arcade gaming

Sega was once a console rival to Nintendo and a giant of the arcade era. With Sonic the Hedgehog and a legacy of bold experiments, Sega remains one of gaming’s most iconic names.
