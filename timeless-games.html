This page explores many iconic and unforgettable games that defined entire generations of players.

PONG

1972

One of the first computer games ever created. Simple, competitive, and surprisingly addictive.

TETRIS

1984

A timeless puzzle game where players arrange falling blocks to clear lines.

Super Mario Bros.

1985

The groundbreaking platformer that transformed side-scrolling games forever.

Sonic the Hedgehog

1991

The debut of Sega’s blue mascot, known for fast-paced platforming action.

Donkey Kong Country

1994

A technical marvel of the SNES era with fluid animation and challenging platforming.

Super Mario 64

1996

The revolutionary 3D platformer that set new standards for game design.

Metal Gear Solid

1998

A cinematic stealth-action game with deep storytelling and tactical gameplay.

Doom

1993

The FPS classic that revolutionized first-person shooters.

Halo: Combat Evolved

2001

The sci-fi shooter that redefined console FPS gameplay and multiplayer.

Portal

2007

A clever puzzle game built on physics-bending portals and dark humor.

Resident Evil 4

2005

A defining survival horror action hybrid with an iconic over-the-shoulder camera.

Bioshock

2007

An atmospheric masterpiece set in the mysterious underwater city of Rapture.

Dark Souls

2011

A brutally challenging action RPG known for its world design and lore.

Minecraft

2011

A global hit centered on creativity, exploration, survival, and building.

Bloodborne

2015

Fast, ruthless, gothic, unforgettable — an action RPG with intense combat.

Red Dead Redemption 2

2018

An epic western adventure with emotional storytelling and vast open-world detail.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

2019

A masterclass in precision swordplay, stealth, and Sengoku-era drama.
